Fancy Ramen

Photo by

Description

A simple ramen packet dish enhanced with vegetables and other ingredients.

Recipe by abrown604

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • ~227 grams (8 ounces) of sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1 bunch of green onions, thinly sliced and divided
  • 1 large carrot, sliced
  • ½ yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of sriracha sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of rice wine vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon of ground white pepper
  • ½ teaspoon of cracked black pepper
  • 2 packages (3 ounces or 85 grams) of chicken-flavoured ramen noodles

Steps

  1. In a large pot, add water, mushroom, half the green onions, carrot, yellow onion, soy sauce, garlic, sriracha sauce, sesame oil, rice wine vinegar, white and black pepper, and flavor packets from the ramen packet. Bring it to a boil, then add the noodles. Cook for about 5 minutes until the noodles are tender yet firm, occasionally stirring.
  2. Reduce heat to a simmer and cook until vegetables are tender for about 10 minutes. Serve topped with remaining green onions.
